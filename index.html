Hello, I'm Mariangel.

My home is Bogota, Colombia.

Background

  • Mariangel is a Venezuelan-born violinist who has been living in Colombia since 2018. she started playing violin herself at the age of five— and has never stopped. Through diligence, ambition and an unflinching dedication to her craft, she has amassed the necessary skill set to educate others, namely children, in the finer art of violin playing.
  • In Venezuela, Ms. Mujica worked with the international organization El Sistema, reflected in children’s musical education for a number of years.
  • Here in Colombia she's continuing her musical studies and teaching where the foundation was set in place for her to fully realize her dream of teaching children as a profession.
  • She is passionate about music and childhood education and is excited to undertake this endeavor of teaching kids and taking care of them in the US.
  • She looks forward to giving kids the same appreciation for the violin, and for classical music, that she learned. Fluent in both English and Spanish, she lives in Colombia, where she teaches violin lessons in English (or Spanish, if you like.!)
